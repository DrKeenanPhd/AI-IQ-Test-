## Project Core Directive (Summary)
- AI-powered IQ test for businesses, delivered via a lead funnel.
- Users interact with an AI agent, who asks questions, enriches the experience, and collects data.
- AI agent creates/updates a contact in GoHighLevel, runs the test, and aggregates results from multiple APIs.
- After the test, the user receives a SmartLink to a secure, personalized results page.
- Results page displays: AI IQ score, summary points, graphical representations, performance/opportunity indices, and solution/ROI tabs.
- Integrates with JSON output from the Vapi AI agent.
